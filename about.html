The Home Page contains 100 different coins with basic information on earch one. Press "More Info" to get a picture of the specific coin & a price of it in $,€ & ₪. You can use "search"button in order to get an information of a specific coin. The whole name of a coin should be written in the search input.

The Live Reports page displays charts with a current price of the coins in $. Charts are updated every 2 seconds. To see a relative chart with a coins you're interested in, you should first select them on the Home Page with a "toggle-button". Only 5 coins can be selected at once. I kindly recommend you to refresh the homepage, before making your choice.
